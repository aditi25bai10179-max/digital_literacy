Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
->Actionable Safety Tips
. Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
. Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking PIN with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
. Be cautious of unknown links, emails, or messages—verify before clicking
. Check privacy settings on your social media accounts and limit what you share publicly
. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email
-> UPI & Financial Safety (Important)
. Never approve a “collect request” or scan a QR code to receive money—this is a common scam
. Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money through UPI apps
. Use official banking or payment apps only; avoid downloading apps from unknown sources
-> General Awareness
. Do not overshare personal information like your address, phone number, or daily routine online
. Regularly update your apps and devices to protect against security vulnerabilities
. Log out from shared or public computers after use
-> Report Cybercrime Immediately
 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Helpline Number: 1930

If you face any cyber fraud, report it quickly to increase the chances of recovering your money.
